## 11. Technical Visual Direction Layer

### Global Technical Direction

#### Environment Direction
Dark studio environment. Charcoal-to-black gradient backgrounds. Dark polished acrylic or marble surfaces for subtle reflections. No natural environments. No bright or white surfaces. The environment exists only to make the Cosmic Orange titanium glow.

#### Lighting Direction
Dramatic key light from high above or 45-degree side angle. Creates rich metallic highlights on the titanium frame edges and camera plateau rim. Secondary subtle fill to prevent crushing details in shadows. The Cosmic Orange must read as a warm metallic orange/copper — not red, not brown. Edge lighting to define the flat titanium sides. No flat even lighting — this is high-contrast dramatic product lighting.

#### Material / Reflection Direction
Titanium flat-edge frame with subtle metallic sheen. Matte textured glass back with glossy Apple logo. Camera plateau has a slightly different finish — polished metallic ring around each lens, dark glass lens elements. The display is black glass when off — deep, dark, mirror-like with minimal reflection. Cosmic Orange color should show warm copper/orange tones in highlights, darker bronze in shadows.

#### Camera / Optics Direction
85mm lens look for full-device shots. Macro lens for detail shots (camera plateau, titanium texture). Full-frame commercial aesthetic. Clean, distortion-free. Slightly longer focal length to compress perspective and make the device look substantial.

#### Composition Direction
Device centered or slightly off-center. Three-quarter angle for hero shots shows both display face and titanium edge. Diagonal placement for dynamic social shots. Camera plateau visible in rear three-quarter shots. Flat lay for UGC-style content. Generous negative space for ad copy on social assets.

#### Color Direction
Cosmic Orange is the hero. Deep metallic orange/copper. Warm but not hot — think sunset metallic, not safety orange. Backgrounds are black to dark charcoal — never compete with the product. Subtle warm bounce light to keep the orange feeling luxurious. No cool/blue tones that would fight the orange.

#### Props Direction
No props. No accessories. No cables. No other devices. The iPhone 17 Pro Max is the complete visual subject.

#### Platform Direction
Mixed: Square (1:1) for Instagram. Vertical (4:5) for social ads. Portrait (9:16) for Stories/Reels. Horizontal for Apple.com hero and premium display ads.

#### Video Motion Direction
Slow, deliberate, premium. Gentle rotation around the vertical axis to reveal titanium edge and Cosmic Orange finish. Slow dolly around the camera plateau. Apple keynote-style reveal pacing. No fast cuts. No jarring movement. Motion should feel expensive and intentional.

#### Continuity Direction
Cosmic Orange color must be identical across all assets and all frames in video. Camera plateau layout unchanged. Titanium frame finish consistent. Display always black/off. Apple logo position fixed.

### Per-Asset Technical Adjustments

#### Product Hero Image
Full device on dark studio surface. Slight three-quarter angle showing display side and titanium edge. Dramatic overhead key light creating edge highlights on the titanium frame. Camera plateau visible on rear shots.

#### Lifestyle Product Image
Device on dark polished surface with subtle reflection beneath. Dark moody atmosphere. Single warm light source creating dramatic shadows. Cosmic Orange is the only color element.

#### Clean E-commerce Image
Dark seamless background. Device centered, slight angle for depth. Even dramatic lighting to show both the display and titanium frame accurately. Maximum product clarity.

#### Paid Social Ad Base Image
Device diagonally placed in frame. Generous negative space in upper or side area for ad copy. Dramatic lighting makes Cosmic Orange pop. Dark background with gradient.

#### Macro / Detail Image
Extreme close-up of the camera plateau — triple-lens array with the Cosmic Orange titanium frame visible around it. Alternatively, close-up of the titanium edge showing the metallic finish texture.

#### Product Reveal Video
Device on dark surface. Slow 360-degree rotation showing front view, titanium edge, back with camera plateau, and return. Gentle and polished.

#### Lifestyle Use-Case Video
Device resting on dark polished surface. Slow dramatic dolly in. Light shifts from side to front revealing the Cosmic Orange. Premium and calm.

#### UGC-Style Product Video
Top-down tabletop view. Device placed on dark surface. Gentle handheld orbit. Natural dramatic window light from one side.

#### Premium Cinematic Product Video
Cinematic dolly around the device starting from camera plateau detail, pulling back to reveal the full device, then orbiting to show the Cosmic Orange titanium frame. Elegant lighting evolution.

#### Short Social Ad Video
Quick dynamic reveal. Device drops or slides into frame. Quick rotation to show color and design. Punchy and engaging.
