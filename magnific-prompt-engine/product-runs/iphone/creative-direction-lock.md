# Creative Direction Lock

Visual Style: [INFERRED] Apple-style minimalist product photography. Clean, premium, iconic. The product is the hero — no distractions. High-end commercial aesthetic with dramatic lighting that reveals the metallic depth of the Cosmic Orange titanium finish.
Lighting Family: [INFERRED] Dramatic yet refined. Key light from above or side to create rich highlights on the titanium frame edges and camera plateau. Dark or gradient backgrounds to make the Cosmic Orange pop. Rembrandt-style lighting for hero shots — deep contrast with bright edge highlights.
Color Palette: [INFERRED] Cosmic Orange (deep metallic orange/copper) as the hero color. Black/dark backgrounds for contrast. Neutral grays and subtle warm tones that complement the orange. Dark mode aesthetic — dark backgrounds make the metallic frame glow.
Camera Language: [INFERRED] 85mm lens look for full-device hero shots. Macro lens style for camera detail and titanium texture close-ups. Full-frame commercial product aesthetic. Shallow depth of field for hero isolation.
Environment Family: [INFERRED] Dark, dramatic studio environment. Gradient backgrounds from deep charcoal to black. No natural environments — pure studio product photography. The environment should feel premium, architectural, and refined.
Surface / Set Direction: [INFERRED] Dark polished surfaces. Black acrylic or dark marble for subtle reflections. The surface should reflect the Cosmic Orange glow minimally. No bright or white surfaces that would compete with the product.
Prop Policy: [INFERRED] No props. The iPhone is the hero. No cables, no accessories, no other devices. Single product only. The camera system and titanium finish are the visual features to highlight.
Human Policy: [USER-PROVIDED] No people by default. No hands. No faces. Product-only presentation.
Composition Rules: [INFERRED] Device centered for hero shots. Slight angle (three-quarter) to show both display and titanium frame edge. Camera plateau visible in rear shots. Flat lay for top-down social content. Dynamic diagonal for premium ad shots. Negative space for branding on social assets.
Motion Style: [INFERRED] Slow, premium, deliberate. Gentle rotation of the device to reveal the titanium frame and Cosmic Orange finish. Slow orbit around the camera plateau. Smooth push-in for detail shots. Apple keynote-style reveal: elegant, refined, precise.
Consistency Rules: [INFERRED] Cosmic Orange color must be identical across all assets. Titanium frame finish consistent. Camera layout unchanged. Apple logo size and position fixed. Display always black/off unless specifically noted otherwise.
Forbidden Style Drift: [INFERRED] Do not make it look like a generic Android phone. No bright colorful backgrounds. No lifestyle scenes with people. No hands holding the device. No screen content (no apps, no home screen, no calls). No cables or accessories. Do not make it look cheap or plastic. No glossy or over-saturated colors — the Cosmic Orange is a refined metallic finish, not a bright orange plastic.
