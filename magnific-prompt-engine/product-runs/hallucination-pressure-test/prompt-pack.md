#### Props Direction
No props. No secondary objects. No surfaces with strong character. Product only.

If motion is used: gentle, stable, slow reveal. Product rotation on vertical axis (if any). No fast camera moves, no shakes, no dramatic motion.

In video: bottle appearance must remain consistent across frames. No shape, color, or packaging changes. Logo position must be stable.
