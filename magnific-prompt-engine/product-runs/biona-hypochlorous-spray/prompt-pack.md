## 11. Technical Visual Direction Layer

### Global Technical Direction

#### Environment Direction
Clean, minimal settings. White or very light cool-toned backgrounds. Bathroom/vanity context for lifestyle shots — clean white marble, subway tile, or fresh towel textures. No clinical/medical environments (no exam tables, white coats, or doctor's office settings). No dark or moody environments.

#### Lighting Direction
Soft, diffused lighting with bright, fresh quality. Skincare photography standard: large softbox or window light. Even illumination with gentle highlights on the bottle surface. Slight cool tint to evoke cleanliness and refreshment. No warm/nostalgic lighting. No hard shadows.

#### Material / Reflection Direction
The bottle is a white matte or semi-matte finish spray container. Gentle, soft reflections rather than sharp specular highlights. Spray nozzle should be visible as a darker/contrasting element on top. Label surface flat with minimal reflection. No mirrored or glassy surfaces on the bottle.

#### Camera / Optics Direction
85mm lens look preferred for hero images. Full-frame commercial product aesthetic. Macro capability for mist/detail shots. Clean, distortion-free. Shallow depth of field for hero/product isolation, deep focus for e-commerce clarity.

#### Composition Direction
Bottle vertical, full-height visible. Spray nozzle at top. Label readable but not overly dominant. For mist shots: focus on the spray burst with bottle out of focus in background. Vertical framing preferred for social, square for e-commerce.

#### Color Direction
White bottle with likely cool-blue or teal brand accents. Clean white balance — slight cool shift acceptable to feel fresh. No warm orange/yellow tones. Mist should appear as clear/transparent fine water particles.

#### Props Direction
Minimal. Gentle skincare context only:
- Clean white folded towel or washcloth
- Single fresh cotton pad
- Clean white marble or quartz surface
- Aloe leaf or simple botanical accent (optional, gentle)
- Water droplets on surface
No medical props (no stethoscopes, no white coats, no clinical instruments). No multiple products (keep single-product focus).

#### Platform Direction
Mixed: Square (1:1) for Instagram feed / Amazon. Vertical (4:5) for social feed ads. Portrait (9:16) for Stories/Reels/TikTok.

#### Video Motion Direction
Calm, spa-like, slow. Gentle product rotation (vertical axis). Slow reveal from low angle. Gentle push-in. If showing mist: slow-motion spray visualization with fine particles catching light. No fast cuts, no jarring motion, no flash transitions.

#### Continuity Direction
Bottle must remain visually consistent across all assets: same color, same label design, same nozzle, same proportions. Label text must not change position or content between frames in video. Mist behavior should be consistent (fine, gentle, dispersed particles).

### Per-Asset Technical Adjustments

#### Product Hero Image
Full bottle centered on clean white surface. Slight angle to show both front label and side. Nozzle clearly visible at top. Soft bright lighting.

#### Lifestyle Product Image
Bottle on clean vanity or bathroom counter. White marble or quartz surface. Folded white towel beside or behind. Gentle natural light. Spa-like calm atmosphere.

#### Clean E-commerce Image
White seamless background. Bottle centered, straight-on or slight angle. Maximum label clarity. Full sharpness. Pure white background with subtle shadow for grounding.

#### Paid Social Ad Base Image
Product off-center (rule of thirds), leaving negative space for ad copy. White/clean background. Soft studio lighting. Bottle tilted slightly for dynamic feel.

#### Macro / Detail Image
Extreme close-up on spray nozzle with fine mist particles visible mid-air. Shallow depth of field. Backlit mist droplets catching light. Bottle body soft in background.

#### Product Reveal Video
Slow smooth vertical orbit around bottle starting from front, moving to side angle. Gentle consistent rotation speed. Label readable throughout.

#### Lifestyle Use-Case Video
Bottle on clean bathroom counter. Gentle handheld feel. Slow pan across setting ending on product. No people. Soft daylight from window.

#### UGC-Style Product Video
Tabletop view looking down at bottle from slightly above. Gentle natural room lighting. Casual handheld stability. Simple clean setting.

#### Premium Cinematic Product Video
Slow polished reveal from behind bottle, arcing around to front. Elegant lighting. Fine water mist particles catching light in background. Premium texture and depth.

#### Short Social Ad Video
Quick punchy format. Bottle appears on screen, quick gentle rotation or slight movement, simple text hook area. Clean and fast.
